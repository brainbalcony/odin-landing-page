Trains are heros

The story of the transporteurs of freedoms.

Where heros came from?

Some thousands years BCE, the first form of a train were actually wagonways pulled by animals.

Then around thousands years later an engine replaced the animal force and the wagons were put on steel tracks.

By the beginning of the past century, railroads were on every continent, even under the Earth, though they still hadn't reached everyone.

Later with support from environmentalist they were ought to become the mass transporteur of passengers.
